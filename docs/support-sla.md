# Support SLA

This support posture applies after a signed customer agreement selects a support
tier. Pilot and design-partner agreements can override these targets.

## Channels

- Customer support portal or shared ticket queue.
- Emergency escalation channel for Premium support.
- Status page for managed SaaS incidents.
- Support bundles generated by `scripts/turbalance-support-bundle.js`.

## Severity

| Severity | Description | Initial Response | Update Cadence |
| --- | --- | ---: | ---: |
| P1 | Production service unavailable, data ingest stopped, or customer-visible security incident | 1 hour | 30 minutes |
| P2 | Major feature degraded, partial ingest/query outage, or blocked production upgrade | 4 business hours | Daily |
| P3 | Non-critical defect, documentation issue, or support question | 1 business day | Weekly |
| P4 | Enhancement request or roadmap discussion | 3 business days | As agreed |

## Customer Responsibilities

- Provide tenant, deployment, version, and support-bundle evidence.
- Rotate secrets and certificates in the customer secret manager when requested.
- Keep customer-managed identity, object storage, metadata DB, queue, and
  registry access available during incident response.

## Turbalance Responsibilities

- Triage by severity and customer impact.
- Preserve audit evidence and keep sensitive values redacted.
- Provide mitigation, rollback, or remediation guidance.
- Publish post-incident follow-up for P1 managed SaaS incidents.
